Frequently Asked Questions

Explore the most common inquiries to learn more about how we support both hosts and renters in finding the perfect rental solutions.

For more inquiries, reach us at contact@shorttie.com.

How do I book an apartment through Shorttiee?

Booking an apartment is simple and straightforward. Browse our listings, select your desired property, choose your dates, and proceed to payment. Once your booking is confirmed, you'll receive all the necessary details about your stay.

What are the benefits of hosting my property on Shorttiee?

Hosting with us gives you access to a large pool of verified renters, professional support, and tools to manage your bookings effortlessly. You'll enjoy seamless payment processing, personalized marketing, and 24/7 customer support to ensure your hosting experience is hassle-free.

Is there a vetting process for renters and hosts?

Yes, we have a thorough vetting process for both renters and hosts to ensure safety and trust. Renters provide identification and payment information, while hosts submit property details and ownership verification. This ensures a secure and reliable platform for everyone.

What happens if there’s an issue during a rental?

Our support team is available 24/7 to handle any issues. Whether you're a host or renter, you can contact us via our help center or customer service hotline. We'll mediate and resolve any disputes quickly and fairly to ensure a smooth experience for all parties involved.
